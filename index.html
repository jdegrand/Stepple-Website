How It Works

Three steps. No gym required.

1

Hatch

Pick a daily goal that fits you and hatch your first egg. It can be as small as a short daily walk.

2

Move

Walk, run, cycle, lift, dance, swim, hike — whatever moves you. Stepple syncs with Apple Health so every bit of activity counts.

3

Thrive

Meet your goal and your pet gains hearts. Miss a day and they lose some — but get moving tomorrow and they bounce right back.

Private by Default.

No account. No sign-up. No ads. By default, your health data is read from Apple Health and stays on your device. Optional social features — if you turn them on — let you share what you choose through Apple's CloudKit service with members of leaderboards you join. Anonymous app-usage events (no health data, no pet names) help improve Stepple — see Processors. If you ever need support, you can choose to share encrypted diagnostic logs — but that's always your call.
